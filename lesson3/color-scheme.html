Site Purpose

The main purpose of the TuClimateTime website is showing the weather of specific places or locations in real Time to users. Users will know when it will a good time to do their outdoor activities.

A secondary purpose of the website is to inform users about possible natural disasters near by them. In this way, many people will not be affected. It might even avoid possible deaths

Users will be able to access it in your cell phone without any problem.

This website will be really simple and easy to understand and use. With graphing and images, users will have a really good idea about the weather

Through time, there will be some changes in website, but it will still work no matter what
